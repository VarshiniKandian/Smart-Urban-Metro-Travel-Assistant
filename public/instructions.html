METRO MATE INSTRUCTIONS

Getting Started

  • 1. Select City: Choose your metro city from the City dropdown to load relevant network data.
  • 2. Pick Stations: Select your From Station and To Station. The system validates your inputs to ensure they’re within the chosen city’s metro map.
  • 3. Start Journey: Click the Start Journey button. The app will instantly calculate the shortest, fastest, and most cost-effective route between the selected stations.
  • 4. Route Visualization: Your route will be displayed on an interactive map with color-coded metro lines, interchange stations, and estimated travel time.

Managing Your Journey

  • Start Journey: Begins the route computation using metro network data and Dijkstra’s Algorithm for optimal pathfinding.
  • Track Fare & Time: Displays estimated fare and travel duration for the complete route, helping you plan efficiently before traveling.
  • End Journey: Marks your travel as complete and logs it securely into your user history for analytics and recordkeeping.
  • Reverse Route: Tap the reverse icon to instantly generate the route from your destination back to your source.

Menu Sidebar (☰)

  • Travel History: Displays your previous metro trips with fare, duration, and route summaries — helping you track commuting habits.
  • Analytics Dashboard: View charts showing your most visited stations, total trips, and fare distribution, powered by your travel data.
  • Settings & Updates: Customize preferences and manage your account securely. Admins can add or update metro station data here.
  • Logout: Use this option to securely sign out and clear your session.
